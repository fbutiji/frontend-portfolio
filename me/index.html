My Cuisines


EKWANG” is a dish very common in the South West Region of Cameroon and is said to have originated from this Region. This dish relished by many and contains the basic classes of food, the flavor and taste are heavenly mouth-watering. It is cheap, fast to prepare and very nutritive. It is usually wrapped in vegetables which are so rich in vitamins.

ACHU AND YELLOW SOUP.This is Achu – pounded cocoyams with a traditional yellow soup. Achu is the traditional meal of a number of villages in the North West region of Cameroon. It is the specialty of the Bamenda or “Bamda” people as I like to say. It is also widely eaten by the Bamilekes in the western region of the country.

KOKI CORN is one of the foods that is popular all over Cameroon. This dish is normally cooked during the raining season because of the abundance of fresh corn, and it is very tasty and delicious.
